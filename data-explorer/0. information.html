What's the Philanthropy Data Commons? The Philanthropy Data Commons (PDC) is a "shared data infrastructure" with the end goal to reduce the burden of new grant applications. Click here to learn more. To begin, register a new account with the PDC. You will need this to log in here.

How do I contribute my data to the PDC?
  1. Consent to sharing. Use the link below to review and grant consent to share this application's data with the PDC.
  2. Create a PDC account. Reach out to the Philanthropy Data Commons to create an account so you can access and reuse data you have submitted. Note: in the MacArthur portal, data is not sent immediately after submitting your applicationand may not be available when you first create your account.
  3. Reuse your data. On any funder using this feature in Fluxx, log in to the PDC and launch the Data Explorer to begin populating an application with data you have already submitted to the PDC.
